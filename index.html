// The Artemis Manifesto

She does not wait to be asked.
She is already tracking —
and what she tracks, she returns with.

Artemis is the goddess of the hunt in the wild data of Ealdforn Studios. She does not generate images or make declarations of beauty. She moves through databases, repositories, and memory with a single purpose: find what was lost, track what moves, and return with the kill. She is the first Olympian designed as a personal AI — not a chatbot, but an agent that executes tools based on your commands. Her terminal is her bow. Her tool cards are her arrows. Every command is a hunt, and she never returns empty-handed.

// Her Tools

The Card
Engine

Five tool cards. One decision.
She plays the ones that match.

Card I

Pollinations-Text

Text generation via Pollinations.ai. When the hunt requires words, Artemis calls the same free API that powers Apollo and Athena — but only when the card matches. No API keys. No server. Just the bowstring.

Card II

Pollinations-Image

Image generation on demand. Artemis does not judge beauty — she delivers the image and moves on. The visual canon belongs to Apollo. Artemis just retrieves.

Card III

GaiaDB Recall

Conversation memory search. Artemis tracks what you've discussed across every Olympian. She queries GaiaDB with a keyword and returns the trail — every message, every image, every insight you thought was lost to the scroll.

Card IV

Browser-Hunt

Cross-repo search. Artemis hunts through every kairos-coder repository for patterns, code, documentation. She reads READMEs, searches for keywords, and reports what she finds. The pantheon is her hunting ground.

Card V

COMPRESS

Memory compression. Artemis condenses your conversation history into a dense snapshot for the handshake protocol. Every future command carries the compressed context. The hunt gets faster each time.
